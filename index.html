Is it good or bad? Welcome to the age of Robotics

A sudden outbreak made us realize how important "efficiency" and "technology" are in front of the virus. Although the epidemic has brought great losses to human beings, it also breeds innovation and opportunities. Recently, many countries have staged scenes of "machine heroes", which play a very important role in the rescue process, from helping medical staff to sort drugs to communicating with patients remotely.


After an epidemic, robots have entered our lives even more.


Let's take a look at the chart:

Picture source | Center for Robot-Assisted Search and Rescue


As of April 20, 16 countries have applied robots in isolation, disinfection, diagnosis, public information dissemination and traffic control, and 7 countries have used robots as "medical assistants" in medical centers Responsible for remote communication, drug transportation and patient consultation and other functions. In addition, seven other countries have put robots into use in infrastructure construction to ensure the quality of life of residents. It can be seen that more and more countries have begun to have the "robot" consciousness and put it into use. In the future, more and more intelligent robots will appear in the fields of public health, public security and infrastructure construction.
